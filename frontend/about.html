About PawsitiveWalks

PawsitiveWalks connects dog owners with walkers through a zero-fee community platform that serves as a simple bridge, not a middleman. Unlike commercial services, our platform displays contact information for direct negotiation with no transaction costs.

How It Works

For Dog Owners

Post requests detailing your dogs and schedules. Find the perfect match for your furry friend's needs.

For Walkers

Students, retirees, or dog enthusiasts can create profiles showcasing availability and experience to start walking.

More Than Just a Service

Beyond professional services, the platform enables dog owners to find other owners for group walks and social meetups.

We also warmly welcome dog lovers without pets to gain hands-on experience and enjoy quality time with dogs.
